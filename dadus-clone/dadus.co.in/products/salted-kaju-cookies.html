About Salted Kaju Cookies – Rich Cashew Biscuit
Salted Kaju Cookies are rich and flavourful biscuits made with premium cashews. Known for their crisp texture and buttery taste, these cookies offer a perfect balance of salt and richness. The presence of kaju adds a nutty flavour, making them a delightful snack for any time of the day. Ideal for tea-time or gifting, these cookies are loved for their smooth bite and satisfying taste.

Salted Kaju Cookies are available in:
250 grams – ₹265

Crafted with Traditional Technique
At Dadus, these cookies are prepared using quality cashews and traditional baking methods to maintain their texture, flavour and consistency.

Taste and Texture Profile
Salted Kaju cookies have a crisp texture with a rich buttery taste and mild salty flavour.
